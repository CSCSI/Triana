Congugate

Author : Ian Taylor
Input Types : ComplexSampleSet and ComplexSpectrum
Output Types : ComplexSampleSet and ComplexSpectrum
Date : 20 May 1997

The Congugate is a unit which takes in a complex number (either a ComplexSpectrum or a ComplexSampleSet) and outputs its complex conjugate.

The Congugate has no parameters to edit and therefore has no unit parameter window.
